### Synthetic Dataset Contribution

- A significant portion of the dataset consists of synthetic academic catalog documents created by the author to simulate realistic and complex academic planning scenarios.

#### These include:

- prerequisite chains (A → B → C dependencies)
- AND / OR conditions (e.g., CS102 AND (MATH101 OR MATH201))
- co-requisite constraints (must be taken concurrently)
- minimum grade requirements (e.g., B or above)
- exceptions (instructor consent overrides)
- program rules (credits, electives, residency requirements)
- ambiguous catalog wording requiring clarification

#### Purpose:
- To enhance dataset diversity, enable multi-hop reasoning, and ensure robust evaluation of prerequisite logic and course planning under incomplete or ambiguous information.
